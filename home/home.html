Digital Time Capsule

Meet Elise Luna,

She is an outgoing latina born and raised in the heart of the Bay Area. As she navigates life in her early 20s as the eldest sibling, she brings a unique perspective shaped by her familial role and life values. Her personality gleams with creativity, empathy, and love and this displays throughout her online presence and digital footprint.


Through her web design project, "Digital Time Capsule", she embarks on a journey of self-discovery and expression. With a keen eye for visual design and a desire to authentically represent herself online, she seeks to carve out a digital space that mirrors her true interests and personality. As she navigates this creative endeavor, Elise aims to not only gain insight into her own digital presence but also to share a piece of herself with visitors.
