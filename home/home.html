Meet Elise Luna,

A vibrant Latinx young woman from the dynamic Bay Area. As the eldest sibling, she navigates life with a unique perspective shaped by her valued family. With a heart brimming with creativity, empathy, and love, Elise finds joy in myriad activities that enrich her life and connect her with others and the world around her.


Elise is well known for her love of exploration. Applying to all aspects, Elise's curiosity extends beyond hobbies; she is always eager to discover new things and experiences, embracing the notion of lifelong learning and self-improvement.

All of which is apparent through her online presence. Whether through watch history, subscriptions, or curated collections she's made, you may find a novel compilation of data.


While Elise enjoys solitary pursuits, she finds equal delight in shared experiences with her loved ones. Whether it's cozying up on the couch for a binge-watch session of the latest shows or embarking on spontaneous road trips accompanied by a curated playlist, she cherishes the moments spent bonding and creating memories with those closest to her.


Through her web design project, "Digital Time Capsule", she embarks on a journey of self-discovery and expression. With a keen eye for design and a desire to authentically represent herself online, she seeks to carve out a digital space that mirrors her genuine interests and personality. As she navigates this creative endeavor, Elise aims to not only gain insight into her own digital footprint but also to share a piece of herself with visitors.
